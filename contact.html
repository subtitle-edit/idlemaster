Frequently Asked Questions

Is Idle Master safe to use?

Yes! Idle Master uses Steam's official API and doesn't modify any game files. It's been trusted by thousands of users worldwide for years.

How do I get started?

Simply download Idle Master, install it, log in with your Steam account, and let it automatically farm trading cards from your library.

Is it completely free?

Yes, Idle Master is completely free and open-source. There are no hidden fees or premium versions.
