About Idle Master

Empowering gamers to maximize their Steam library value

Who We Are

Idle Master is a powerful automation tool designed for Steam gamers who want to maximize the value of their game libraries. We understand that many users have extensive collections of games with untapped trading card potential, and we've created the perfect solution to help you farm these cards effortlessly.

Built by gamers for gamers, Idle Master simplifies the process of earning Steam trading cards. Our tool runs seamlessly in the background, allowing you to collect cards without the need to manually launch or play games. Whether you're a casual player or a dedicated collector, Idle Master helps you unlock the full potential of your Steam library.

Our Story

Idle Master was born from a simple problem: many Steam users had large libraries but lacked the time to manually farm trading cards. The original creators recognized that the existing solutions were either complicated, unsafe, or inefficient.

What started as a small open-source project quickly gained traction among the gaming community. Gamers appreciated the simplicity, security, and effectiveness of the tool. Over time, Idle Master evolved with new features, improved performance, and a growing user base.

Today, Idle Master is trusted by thousands of users worldwide and continues to be actively developed and maintained. We're proud to be the go-to solution for automated Steam trading card farming, and we're committed to serving our community for years to come.
